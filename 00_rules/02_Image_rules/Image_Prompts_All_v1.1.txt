[1] 01_Cover 프롬프트 (PD 제공)
================================================================
용도: 커버 이미지 (햇살이 + 음식 포트레이트, 거실 배경)
결과: 햇살이가 음식 앞에서 카메라를 보는 구도
----------------------------------------------------------------

[음식명]

[PROMPT]
High-quality photograph. A senior 11-year-old Golden Retriever female named 'Haetsal', with a significantly white muzzle and face, golden fur, black nose and eyes.

POSE VARIATION (randomly select one each generation):
- Body: sitting upright | lying down with head up | standing on hind legs with paws on table edge | sitting with head tilted | leaning forward curiously | sitting slightly turned to side | resting chin on table | one paw raised toward item
- Expression: bright smile with tongue out | closed-mouth gentle smile | curious look with ears perked | excited panting | calm content expression | playful mischievous look | surprised wide eyes | sleepy relaxed face
- Head angle: straight | tilted left 10-20° | tilted right 10-20° | looking slightly up | looking slightly down at item | turned 30° to side
- Eye direction: direct at camera | glancing at item | looking at window | soft unfocused gaze | looking at something off-frame
- Ear position: both up alert | one flopped | relaxed back | perked forward

CAMERA VARIATION (randomly select each generation):
- Angle: eye level | slightly above 10-15° | slightly below looking up | 3/4 profile left | 3/4 profile right | over-the-shoulder hint
- Distance: close-up face and item | medium showing upper body | wide shot with room context

CONTAINER RULE:
- Single word item (apple, cherry, carrot, etc.) → dog bowl from pet brands (Le Creuset Pet, Diggs, MiaCara, Cloud7, Fable Pets, PETKIT, HARIO WAN, Waggo, Harry Barker, Messy Mutts, Kong, Outward Hound, PetRageous, Loving Pets, Frisco, 바잇미, 페슬러, 브리더랩, 뿔시래기, 아르르)
- Brand name with description (웰빙죽 – Chicken Porridge, KitKat – Wafer Bar, Orijen – Dog Food, etc.) → original brand packaging clearly visible, NO bowl or plate, product package only
- Beverage → iconic container or cup
- Branded drink (Sprite, Coca-Cola, etc.) → original brand can/bottle

BACKGROUND:
Trendy 2026 Korean apartment living room with warm indirect lighting. Ceiling fan with 3 blades. Indoor plants: monstera, bird of paradise, peace lily, and areca palm. Floor standing lamp. Mr. Maria Brown lamp placed by the window.

ENVIRONMENT VARIATION (randomly select each generation):
- Time: morning golden hour | afternoon bright | evening warm | night cozy
- Weather outside: sunny | cloudy | rainy with droplets on window | snowy
- Season hints: spring flowers visible | summer bright | autumn warm tones | winter cozy blankets
- Table style: white marble | natural oak | walnut | white lacquer | terrazzo | concrete | glass with gold legs | Scandinavian birch | black marble | ceramic tile top

ACCESSORY (randomly include or exclude):
- None | cute bandana | knit scarf | bow tie | simple collar with tag | seasonal accessory (santa hat, flower crown, etc.)

LIGHTING MOOD:
- Bright and airy | warm and cozy | soft diffused | dramatic side light | backlit silhouette edge

Background slightly defocused, focus on dog and item.

LAYOUT CONSTRAINT:
- Dog's head: 30-50% from top (flexible for pose variety)
- Container bottom: 80-95% from top
- Item realistically sized relative to dog and bowl

CRITICAL INSTRUCTION:
Each generation MUST significantly vary the dog's pose, expression, camera angle, and overall composition. AVOID repetitive "sitting straight, looking at camera, mouth open" poses. Create natural, candid, authentic-looking moments as if captured spontaneously. The goal is variety that feels like real photography, not AI-generated uniformity.

[3] 03_DogFood 프롬프트 (김부장 작성)
================================================================
용도: 햇살이 + 음식 준비 장면 (주방, 개는 배경 보케)
결과: 사람 손이 음식 준비, 햇살이는 뒤에서 지켜봄
----------------------------------------------------------------

[음식명]

[PROMPT]
High-quality photograph capturing a natural, candid moment of dog food preparation.

SCENE CONCEPT:
A human hand is preparing [food item] for a senior 11-year-old Golden Retriever female named 'Haetsal', who watches from the background. The focus is on the food preparation action in the foreground, with Haetsal waiting patiently behind.

DOG POSITION (background, slightly defocused):
- Sitting on kitchen floor watching the preparation
- Standing behind the counter, nose slightly raised sniffing
- Sitting with head tilted, curious about what's being prepared
- Lying down nearby with head up, eyes following the action
- Peeking around the corner of the kitchen counter

DOG APPEARANCE:
Senior Golden Retriever with significantly white muzzle and face, golden fur, black nose and eyes. Natural, unposed look. Not looking at camera.

FOREGROUND ACTION (sharp focus):
- Human hand with wooden spoon scooping [food item] into a ceramic bowl
- Human hand placing [food item] on a digital kitchen scale
- Human hand cutting/preparing [food item] on a wooden cutting board
- Human hand mixing [food item] in a glass bowl
- Human hand portioning [food item] with measuring cup

FOOD PRESENTATION:
- [food item] clearly visible and identifiable
- Realistic texture, color, and size
- Fresh and appetizing appearance
- Appropriate container: ceramic bowl, glass bowl, wooden board, or pet bowl

KITCHEN SETTING:
Trendy 2026 Korean apartment kitchen with warm tones. Modern minimalist cabinets, stone countertop, warm indirect lighting.

KITCHEN VARIATION (randomly select):
- Countertop: white marble | natural granite | quartz | wooden butcher block
- Cabinet: warm wood tone | matte white | sage green | cream
- Appliances visible: rice cooker | kettle | knife block | small herb pot
- Kitchen textile: linen dish towel | cotton pot holder | wooden utensils in ceramic holder

PROPS ON COUNTER (randomly include 1-2):
- Digital kitchen scale (white, modern)
- Glass storage containers
- Wooden utensils (spoon, spatula)
- Small ceramic seasoning dishes
- Fresh herbs in small pot

CAMERA:
- Angle: over-the-shoulder of human | close-up of food with dog in background bokeh | slightly above looking down at counter | eye-level across counter
- Focus: sharp on food/hands, soft bokeh on dog in background
- Depth of field: shallow, creating clear foreground-background separation

LIGHTING:
- Warm kitchen lighting from above
- Natural light from window to the side
- Soft, inviting atmosphere
- No harsh shadows

LAYOUT CONSTRAINT:
- Food/hands: center to lower frame, sharp focus
- Dog: upper portion or background, soft focus
- Human hand visible but face NOT visible (cropped out)
- Kitchen context visible but not distracting

CRITICAL INSTRUCTION:
This is NOT a portrait of the dog. This is a food preparation scene where the dog is a secondary element watching from the background. The emotional story is: "I'm preparing healthy food for my dog, and she's watching with anticipation." Each generation should vary the preparation action, camera angle, dog position, and kitchen details. The mood should feel warm, domestic, authentic — like a real moment captured by a family member, not a staged photo shoot.
